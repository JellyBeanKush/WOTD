# QUIDNUNC

*[[KWID-nuhnk]] (noun)*

**Definition**
> A person who is eager to know the latest news and gossip; a busybody.

**Example**
*The streamer's chat was full of quidnuncs desperately trying to leak the upcoming patch notes before the official reveal.*
